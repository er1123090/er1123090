I work at the intersection of Natural Language Processing, Human-Computer Interaction, and empirical AI alignment. My current research focuses on LLM-as-a-Judge, rubric-based evaluation, reward signal reliability, and memory-based preference inference for tool-calling agents.

## Research Interests

- LLM evaluation and LLM-as-a-Judge
- Rubric-based evaluation and reward signal reliability
- Memory-augmented agents and personalized tool calling
- Human-centered dialogue systems
